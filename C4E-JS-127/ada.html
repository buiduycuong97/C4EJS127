76 – 108 Andante Knit Hoodie

This season, we had the pleasure of working with new factories that specialize in sustainable fabrics in pursuit of a healthier and cleaner environment. The Andante Knit Hoodie is constructed with 100% Recycled Plastic Bottle and individually knitted by hand. The hoodie features 2 hidden side pockets and anti-pilling technology to promote longevity of the garment. Each piece is dyed in one of two colors: Hazel Brown or Pewter Blue. The cut of the hoodie is slightly oversized. We recommend to go true to size or to size down for a fitted look.

Body: 100% recycled polyester knit
